Success Stories

From tales of tradition, from dusty workshops and the smell of the earth,E-Art was born. It was shaped by the potter, on his wheel and by the carpenter’s steady hand. By the prick of the needle and the chisel on stone. This is the world where our stories are told. In places and practices from time immemorial, in folklore and fables that have somehow been forgotten. Handcrafted to glorious imperfection, each piece in the E-Art collection recalls the story of it’s own identity. E-Art brings you premium lifestyle products inspired by folklore and traditional crafts. Our products are handcrafted by artisans who have honed their skills over generations of practice. Each product in the E-Art collection is designed with intricate care and concern. Concern for tradition, concern for the material and concern for the artisans. Through each product, E-Art strives to revive what has been lost – the exquisite splendour of traditional arts, the cultural heritage of generations of craftspeople and the memory of age-old stories, embedded in traditional wisdom and folklore.
